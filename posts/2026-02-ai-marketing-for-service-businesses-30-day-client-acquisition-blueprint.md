Service businesses often have strong delivery but inconsistent demand systems. AI marketing can close that gap quickly when execution is structured.

## Week 1: Define signal and offer clarity

- Identify high-value service lines.
- Define ICP and disqualifiers.
- Build offer messaging around business outcomes.

## Week 2: Launch targeted acquisition

- Deploy high-intent search and local demand campaigns.
- Use AI to produce variant copy by segment.
- Track lead quality, not just click volume.

## Week 3: Automate follow-up and qualification

- Implement first-response automation.
- Add qualification prompts before booking.
- Route leads based on urgency and fit.

## Week 4: Optimize and scale

- Review conversion bottlenecks.
- Expand highest-performing segments.
- Set weekly KPI review routines.

## KPI set

Track:

- Cost per qualified lead.
- Time to first response.
- Booked call rate.
- Close rate by source.

## Final note

A 30-day AI marketing system can produce momentum quickly when strategy, operations, and messaging stay aligned.
